Mango Smoothie

About this recipe

This is a simple, but delicious recipe, for a mango and banana smoothie.

Enjoy the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded, and sliced
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 cups vanilla yogurt
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Combine mangos, banana, vanilla yogurt, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
